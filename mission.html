Mission

Reduce the weight people carry.

The squirrels believe that anxiety, anger, and sadness are often intensified by bad systems: unclear choices, hostile interfaces, repeated friction, and tools that demand too much from already tired humans.

Viva Squirrel exists to arrange small things better. Not dramatically. Not loudly. Precisely enough that life becomes a little lighter.
Calm

Interfaces should lower pressure, not perform complexity.

Respect

People are not funnels, targets, cohorts, or metrics. They are tired creatures trying to get through the day.
